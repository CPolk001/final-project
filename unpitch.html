Uses


Unpitched percussion is what most people are familiar with. The standard drumset,
used in many contexts and musical selections, is probably the most recognized
and utilized sound in any piece. Other common unpitched instruments include
the whipcrack (famous in the hit holiday tune, Sleigh Ride), sleighbells, rainstick,
and brake drum. This is the category that provides common and unique sound
effects in addition to keeping the beat.
