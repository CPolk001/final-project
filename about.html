What exactly is percussion?


Percussion is the umbrella term referring to all types of audible instruments that aren't solely powered by wind (with some exceptions).
For example, the five main categories of music-producing instruments are woodwinds, brass, percussion, strings, and voice. However,
there are some unique aspects to percussion that allow for use of wind or even bows, which is what many string instruments use to produce sound.
Below are the four percussion categories with some examples of what an instrument in that category might look like. More information about
each category, including sound and image samples, is provided via hyperlink, or they can be accessed at the top via the navigation bar.
