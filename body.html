Uses


Percussion music sometimes calls for the body to be used. In many instances,
the body creates the perfect sound effect for any type of music or scene. This is often
seen when a slap is needed for comedic effect, or stomping to imitate someone walking with boots. Most percussionists agree that being able to use their own body is a fun
and slightly rewarding opportunity. There are practically no limits to the sound the human body can produce.
